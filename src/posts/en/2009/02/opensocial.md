The <a target="_blank" href="http://codezine.jp/devsumi/2009">Developer Summit 2009</a> will be held at Meguro Gajoen on February 12th and 13th.

In particular, pay close attention to the <a target="_blank" href="http://www.seshop.com/event/dev/2009/timetable/Default.asp?mode=detail&#038;eid=124&#038;sid=744&#038;tr=07%5FDevelopment+Style%28Web%29#744">OpenSocial panel discussion starting at 17:40 on the 12th, which will make big predictions about the future in 2009, the first year of OpenSocial adoption.

I will also be appearing as a panelist, along with Yoichiro Tanaka, who recently wrote "Introduction to OpenSocial", Ariaki Kawasaki from Recruit, Hiroyuki Koyama from mixi, Naoki Ishihara, Shunpei Shiraishi, and Rin Yano from Google.

This year marks the beginning of the launch of multiple OpenSocial environments in Japan, including mixi, goo, and OpenPNE. We plan to discuss the role that OpenSocial will play in the future of the Web. If you are interested, please join us.
